About YALL

YALL is a veteran-owned consultancy focused on software development, AI integration, and secure infrastructure. We help organizations design, build, and deploy intelligent systems that operate in secure, resource-constrained, or disconnected environments.

Our mission is to empower users with secure, private, and self-hosted AI solutions — tailored to meet operational needs in sensitive, edge, and air-gapped deployments.

Founder Background

With over 20 years of experience in network and security engineering, our founder brings deep expertise in secure communications, infrastructure operations, and cyber defense — from combat zones to highly classified environments.

A Marine Corps OIF veteran with hands-on experience in COMSEC operations, CCI handling, and global network defense, he currently holds an active TS/SCI clearance.

Today, he focuses on integrating local LLMs into self-hosted applications and workflows, prioritizing security-first design, hardened infrastructure, and transparent, decentralized systems. He’s a longtime supporter of free and open-source software, active in FOSS communities across Discord, Telegram, and beyond.
